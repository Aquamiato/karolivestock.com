How We Deliver

Truck. Ship. Air.

🚛

Truck (TIR)

Full TIR truckloads direct from European farms. Livestock transport vehicles certified to EU animal welfare standards. Door-to-border delivery with all customs paperwork handled.

Capacity: 200–300 sheep / 40–60 cattle per truck

🚢

Ship (Container)

Large-scale livestock shipments via sea freight. Standard and livestock container vessels from European ports to Gulf and Middle Eastern ports. Most cost-effective for large volumes.

Best for: 500+ animals, bulk orders

✈️

Air Freight

Fast delivery for urgent or high-value orders. Specially equipped cargo aircraft for live animal transport. Full IATA compliance, veterinary supervision throughout the flight.

Best for: urgent, premium or small batches
